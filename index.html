ℹ️ About This Project

Why We Built ClassicaLens

Classical music traditions worldwide are at risk of fading into obscurity. Younger audiences find them inaccessible — not because the music isn't beautiful, but because there's no bridge from curiosity to understanding.

ClassicaLens was built to be that bridge. By combining audio analysis with a rich cultural knowledge base and beginner-friendly explanations, we make it possible for anyone — regardless of background or training — to genuinely connect with classical music heritage.
